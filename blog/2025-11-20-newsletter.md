This update brings several improvements to molecule management in NMRium, making structure creation, labeling, and importing smoother and more intuitive.

1. **Assignment links multiplets to atoms in the molecule.** Custom labels if defined appear in the spectrum, making reporting straightforward.

![assignment links multiplets](/newsletters/2025/november/assignment-links-multiplets.gif)

2. **Fast atom labelling is now available** â€” just press space or enter a custom label to number or name atoms directly, making assignment in natural product studies quick and consistent.

![fast atom labelling](/newsletters/2025/november/fast-atom-labelling.gif)

3. **Molecule editing is easier than ever.** New canvas-level shortcuts let you build and adjust structures efficiently. Check the documentation for tips and details.

![molecule editing](/newsletters/2025/november/molecule-editing.gif)

4. **Molfile imports has been improved to deal with custom atom labels.** NMRium recognizes atom labels that are placed in ZZC, 'A' alias or 'V' value molfile fields
